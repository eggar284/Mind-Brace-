Who We Are

Mindbrace is a company founded by visionary young minds passionate about changing the future of mental health. We believe that technology can become a powerful ally in emotional wellbeing — not only treating but proactively preventing mental health crises.

Our Origins

Born from a group of students, dreamers, and engineers, Mindbrace started as a vision to build a better future — where emotional support is not a luxury, but a standard. Each bracelet we design is a step toward a world where mental health is treated with the urgency, science, and empathy it deserves.

Driven by Innovation

We combine neuroscience, biometric sensors, and intelligent algorithms to create an everyday wearable that listens to your body — and cares for your mind. We work closely with psychologists, engineers, and designers to deliver technology that actually makes a difference.

Empathy is our Core

We believe in real conversations, in listening to pain, and in building tools that genuinely help. Our entire approach is rooted in empathy — for every user, for every life story.
